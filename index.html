Flâpé Pâté is a version of Dong Nguyen's Flappy Bird in a Critical Role setting.

How to play?
Fly through the cave without hitting rock formations.
Press SPACE, UP, W or tap the screen to flâp Pâté's wings.

The game is hosted as an open-source project on GitHub.
